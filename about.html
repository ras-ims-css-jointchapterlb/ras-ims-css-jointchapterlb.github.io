About IC2AI

International Conference on Control, Automation and Instrumentation

About the Conference

The International Conference on Control, Automation, and Instrumentation (IC2AI) is the official scientific conference of the IEEE Joint RA/IM/CS Lebanon Chapter and IFAC Lebanon AMO. The conference aims to bring together scientists, engineers, educators, and practitioners from around the world to present their latest research and applications in control, automation, and instrumentation.

IC2AI serves as an international forum for exchanging ideas, discussing emerging challenges, and fostering collaborative research projects across academia and industry. Distinguished researchers in engineering will be invited as keynote speakers, and accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore.

Scope

The conference covers a wide spectrum of topics including but not limited to control systems theory, industrial automation, robotics, mechatronics, sensors and actuators, instrumentation, artificial intelligence, machine learning in control, and the Internet of Things.

Review Process

All submitted papers undergo a rigorous double-blind peer-review process. Each paper is reviewed by a minimum of two experts from the Technical Program Committee. Evaluation criteria include technical merit, novelty, soundness of results, and clarity of presentation.
