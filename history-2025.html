IC2AI 2025

First International Conference on Control, Automation and Instrumentation

IC2AI 2025 — Beirut, Lebanon

February 11–13, 2025 · La Sagesse University, Beirut

The inaugural edition of IC2AI was held at the Faculty of Engineering, La Sagesse University, Lebanon. The conference successfully brought together researchers and practitioners from across the globe to present advances in control, automation, and instrumentation.

IC2AI 2025 was organized by the IEEE Joint RA/IM/CS Lebanon Chapter and the Lebanese IFAC Chapter and cosponsored by IEEE and IFAC. The proceedings are archived in IEEE Xplore.
