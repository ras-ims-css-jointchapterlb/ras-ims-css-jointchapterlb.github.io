Venue & Hotel

Lebanese American University, Byblos Campus

Lebanese American University (LAU)

Byblos Campus · Blat, Jbeil, Lebanon

IC2AI 2027 will be hosted at the prestigious Byblos Campus of the Lebanese American University. Set on a hillside overlooking the historic city of Byblos and the Mediterranean Sea, the campus provides a state-of-the-art academic environment with modern conference facilities.

Byblos (Jbeil) is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Conference participants will have the opportunity to explore the ancient Phoenician port, the Crusader castle, and the vibrant old souks — all just minutes from the venue.

Address

LAU Byblos Campus, Blat, Jbeil (Byblos), Lebanon

Location Map

Getting There

Beirut–Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY) is the main gateway, approximately 45 km south of the venue. Taxis and shuttle services are available. The drive to Byblos takes 45–60 minutes.

Accommodation

Several 4-star and 5-star hotels are located near the LAU campus. Special conference rates will be negotiated. A recommended hotel list will be published closer to the event.

Visa Requirements

Many nationalities require a visa to enter Lebanon. Please check requirements early and plan accordingly. See Visa Information →
