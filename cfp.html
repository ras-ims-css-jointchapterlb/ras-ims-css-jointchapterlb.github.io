Call for Papers

Scope, Topics of Interest & Submission Guidelines

Scope & Call for Papers

IC2AI 2027 invites researchers, engineers, and practitioners to submit original, unpublished papers describing advances in control, automation, and instrumentation. The conference welcomes both theoretical contributions and applied research with practical impact.

All submitted papers will be peer-reviewed by at least two members of the Technical Program Committee. Accepted and presented papers (with completed registration) will be submitted for inclusion in the conference proceedings on IEEE Xplore.

Topics of Interest

  • Control Systems and Theory
  • Industrial Automation
  • Robotics
  • Process Control
  • Mechatronics
  • Sensors and Actuators
  • Instrumentation
  • Artificial Intelligence in Control
  • Machine Learning in Control
  • Industrial Networking & IoT
  • Human-Machine Interaction
  • Fractional Control Systems
  • Energy Management and Control
  • Cybersecurity in Automation
  • Biomedical Instrumentation
  • Education in Control

Paper Format

Papers must be submitted in PDF format following the IEEE two-column conference template. Papers should be between 4 and 6 pages, including figures, tables, and references.
