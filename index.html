Welcome to IC2AI 2027

The Second International Conference on Control, Automation, and Instrumentation (IC2AI'27) brings together researchers, engineers, and professionals to present their latest advances in control, automation, and instrumentation. The conference serves as a premier platform for knowledge exchange at national and international levels, fostering collaborative research across academia and industry.

Following the success of the inaugural edition held at La Sagesse University, Beirut in February 2025, IC2AI 2027 will be hosted at the Lebanese American University, Byblos Campus. Renowned scholars in engineering and related fields will be invited as keynote speakers. IC2AI'27 is the flagship official scientific conference of the IEEE Joint RA/IM/CS Lebanon Chapter and Lebanese IFAC Chapter.

Spanning three days, the event will feature peer-reviewed paper presentations, special sessions, and tutorials. Accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore.
