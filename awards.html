Conference Awards

Recognizing excellence in research at IC2AI 2027

Awards Program

IC2AI 2027 will recognize outstanding contributions to research in control, automation, and instrumentation through a set of prestigious awards. Award winners will be announced and honored at the closing ceremony on March 19, 2027.

Best Paper Award

Awarded to the paper that best demonstrates original, high-quality research contribution with clear significance to the field.

Best Student Paper Award

Awarded to the best paper with a student as first and presenting author. Students must indicate eligibility during submission.

Best Presentation Award

Recognized by session chairs for exceptional clarity, delivery, and ability to engage the audience during the oral presentation.

Best Poster Award

Awarded to the poster that best communicates research findings visually and facilitates meaningful discussion.

Eligibility & Process
  • All accepted and presented papers are automatically considered for Best Paper.
  • Student Paper Award requires first author to be a student — declare during submission.
  • Finalists are selected by the Technical Program Committee.
  • Winners receive a certificate and a monetary prize.
